Yichen Li

I am a Ph.D. student at MIT CSAIL, advised by Prof. Wojciech Matusik and Prof. Antonio Torralba. My research interests lie in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Robotics. In particular, I am interested in building intelligent systems for multimodal content (vision, touch, and sound) understanding and creation through representation learning, dynamic interactions and physics-based geometry analysis.

I received my masters degree in CS at Stanford, where I have been fortunate to worked with Prof. Leonidas Guibas, and Prof. Gordon Wetzstein. I also worked with Prof.Emily Whiting and Prof. Kate Saenko at Boston University.
